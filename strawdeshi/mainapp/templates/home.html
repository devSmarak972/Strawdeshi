Our Work

What We do

At Strawdeshi, we are revolutionizing the fashion industry by harnessing the power of rice straw from Indian paddy fields, preventing waste and reducing environmental impact. Using our verified process, the straw is retted, dried, and separated into fibers. These fibers are transformed into non-woven insulation. Our sustainable insulation is then used in jackets, pillows, blankets, and more, reducing environmental impact in the fashion industry. Join us in our mission to make a positive impact, one straw at a time.

  • Rice straw collection to reduce waste

  • Conversion of straw into sustainable fiber

  • Refinement for improved uniformity and performance

  • Eco-friendly alternatives: jackets, pillows, blankets

Our Sustainable Impact

Rice straw is a valuable resource that can support India by increasing its local energy supply while reducing its reliance on imports. We aim to utilize 5.4M tons of rice straw. 1 ton of rice straw burnt releases the equivalent of 1521.53 Kg CO2. Hence we will reduce around 8.2M tons of CO2 equivalent annually. This is roughly equal to the carbon footprint of Ottawa, Canada. Unlike cotton, we are not utilizing large quantities of water to manufacture our product. Our filling is completely biodegradable, unlike polyester and microplastics. Furthermore, depending on the market price of rice straw, farmers can expect to earn between INR 550 and 1500 per ton of rice straw sold.
